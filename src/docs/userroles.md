## Student - Role

- Can view their own attendance records.
- Receives notifications about their attendance status.
- May have limited access to certain features based on institutional policies.
- Can generate reports on their attendance history for personal reference.
- Can access the system through a mobile-friendly interface for on-the-go access to attendance records and management.
- May have access to multi-language support to cater to diverse linguistic needs.
- May have the option to integrate with biometric attendance systems for enhanced security and accuracy in attendance recording.
- Can utilize cloud-based storage solutions for secure and scalable storage of attendance data, allowing for easy access and retrieval from any location with internet connectivity.
- Benefits from automatic backup and recovery mechanisms to safeguard attendance data against loss or corruption, ensuring data integrity and continuity of operations.
- Has role-based access control to ensure that they only have access to the features and data relevant to their role, enhancing security and preventing unauthorized access to sensitive information.
- Can view a comprehensive audit trail of all attendance-related activities, providing transparency and accountability in the attendance management process.
- May have access to customizable attendance policies defined by the institution, ensuring that the system can adapt to different institutional contexts and policies.
